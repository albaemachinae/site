The Machinae Albae Privacy Policy and Terms of Use.

Effective as of July 26, 2021

Last updated July 26, 2021


Hi! We are Machinae Albae, a software development and research company. In Machinae Albae we protect your data and respect your privacy.


Actually, we do not track you when you visit our site. This site is for your information only, we don’t use any third-party services to get information about your behavior - we don’t care.


When we talk about the Machinae Albae, we mean this our company Machinae Albae LLC, located in Voronezh, Russia, operating under Russian Law.


You can read this Privacy Policy – it’s not that hard.


If you are a Machinae Albae customer you can use your email and password to get your order data, or if you are just a visitor to our website, please also check our Term of Use.


Sensitive information. We do not collect any "sensitive personal information" about you, we even have no intention to do so.


What about children’s data?


Even if you are a child – we have no intention to detect this fact. Please be a good boy or girl (or anyone you want to be, perhaps a banana) and ask your parents for permission to do any action here.


How and why we use your data.


We only use your information for some specific purposes and where we have a legal basis to do so: if we have a business with you, we will give you a login and password to get to your order details.


Legal basis for Personal Data processing.


We process your data only if you have given us your consent for a specific purpose and you have the freedom to withdraw that consent freely at any time.


Your rights regarding our use of your Personal Data.


  • You can choose not to provide us with personal data. You can turn off cookies in your browser by changing its settings.
  • You have the right to access the information we hold about you.
  • You have the right to make us correct any wrong personal data about you.
  • You have the right to port your data.
  • You have the right to erase any personal data we hold about you if it is no longer necessary for us (except for personal information that we are required by law to keep).
  • You have the right to complain about our use of your data.

How safe is the data we collect?


We have physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to secure and protect the information we collect.


Where do we keep the data?


The data we collect is processed at our VPS services located in Russian Federation.


How long do we store your data?


We will delete and stop actively using your Personal Data within 6 months from the last time you contact us (except for personal information that we are required by law to keep).


Cookies.


Unless you adjust your browser settings to refuse cookies, we can use cookies when you interact with our site - this might be session cookies, meaning they do not delete after returning, or persistent cookies that help us recognize you when you return so we can provide a tailored service.


Your Rights under EU GDPR.


Under the EU General Data Protection Regulation, individuals have the right to request access to and rectification or erasure of their Personal Data, data portability, restriction of processing of your Personal Data. You also have the right to object to the processing of your Personal Data. By emailing support@machinaealbae.com, you can request all your personal data deletion or information in connection with any of your rights under GDPR. If you are a European Union resident, you can file a complaint with a supervisory authority in your Member State.


Your Rights under CCPA.


Under the California Consumer Privacy Act of 2018 (CCPA), individuals have the right to request information about how we have collected, used, and shared and used your personal information during the past 12 months, to request a copy of the personal information that we maintain about you, to delete the personal information that we collected or maintain about you. By emailing support@machinaealbae.com, you can request all your personal data deletion or information in connection with any of your rights under CCPA.


Privacy Policy Updates.


We may update this Privacy Policy; you can see when this Privacy Policy was last revised by referring to the date at the top of the Privacy Policy that is available on our website and mobile app. We may be required by law to obtain your consent concerning these changes.


How to contact us?


Please direct any questions or comments to support@machinaealbae.com
