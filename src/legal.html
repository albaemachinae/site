The Machinae Albae Privacy Policy and Terms of Use.

Effective as of July 26, 2021

Last updated July 26, 2021


Hi! We are Machinae Albae, a software development and research company. In Machinae Albae we protect your data and respect your privacy.


Actually, we do not track you when you visit our site. This site is for your information only, we don’t use any third-party services to get information about your behaviour — we just don’t care.


When we talk about Machinae Albae, we mean our company Machinae Albae LLC, located in Voronezh, Russia and operating under Russian law.


Please do read this Privacy Policy – it’s not that hard.


If you are a Machinae Albae customer, you can use your email and password to view your order details. If you are just visiting our website, please also check our Terms of Use.


Sensitive information. We do not collect any sensitive personal information from you and have no intention whatsoever of doing so.


What about children’s data?


Even if you are a child, we have no intention of detecting this fact. Please be a good girl or boy (or anyone you want to be, perhaps, a banana) and ask your parents for permission to use this website.


How and why we use your data


We only use your information for specific purposes only, when there is a clear legal basis for doing so; such as if we do business with you, we will give you a login and password so that you can get to your order details.


Legal Basis For Processing Personal Data


We process your personal data only if you have given us your consent to use it for a specific purpose, and you have the freedom to withdraw that consent at any time.


Your rights regarding our use of your Personal Data:


  • You can choose not to provide us with personal information. You can turn off cookies in your browser settings.
  • You have the right to access the information we hold about you.
  • You have the right to have the data corrected if any personal data we hold about you is inaccurate.
  • You have the right to port your data.
  • You have the right to erase any personal data we hold about you if it is no longer necessary in relation to the purposes for which it was collected (except for personal information that we are required to keep by law).
  • You have the right to complain about our use of your data.

How safe is your data with us?


We have physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to secure and protect the information we collect.


Where do we keep the data?


The data we collect is processed at our VPS services located in the Russian Federation.


How long do we store your data?


We will delete and stop actively using your Personal Data within 6 months from the last time you contact us (except for personal information that we are required to keep by law).


Cookies


Unless you adjust your browser settings to refuse cookies, we can use cookies when you interact with our site — these might be either session cookies, meaning they are automatically deleted when you quit your browser, or persistent cookies that help us recognize you when you return so we can provide a tailored service.


Your Rights under EU GDPR


Under the EU General Data Protection Regulation, individuals have the right to request access to and rectification or erasure of their Personal Data, data portability, restriction of processing of your Personal Data. You also have the right to object to the processing of your Personal Data. By emailing support@machinaealbae.com, you can ask for all your personal data to be deleted or request information in connection with any of your rights under GDPR. If you are a European Union resident, you can file a complaint with a supervisory authority in your Member State.


Your Rights under CCPA


Under the California Consumer Privacy Act of 2018 (CCPA), individuals have the right to request information about how we have collected, used, and shared your personal information during the past 12 months, to request a copy of the personal information that we maintain about you, to delete the personal information that we collected or maintain about you. By emailing support@machinaealbae.com, you can ask for all your personal data to be deleted or request information in connection with any of your rights under CCPA.


Privacy Policy Updates


We may update this Privacy Policy; you can see when this Privacy Policy was last revised by referring to the date at the top of the Privacy Policy that is available on our website and mobile app. We may be required by law to obtain your consent concerning these changes.


How to contact us?


Please direct any questions or comments to support@machinaealbae.com
